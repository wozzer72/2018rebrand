Keep Fighting the Fight

2020 was a tough year for everyone, dominanted by the Covid 19 global pandemic. Always changing restrictions and lockdowns.

We remember those who lost their fight against the virus and we clapped every Thursday for our NHS heroes. As we start 2021, we have two vaccines to help with the fight.

The vaccine is a great tool. But we need to keep fighting the fight.

Keep washing our hands. Keep covering our face. Keep a safe distance.

Support Our NHS

Do you remember in 2020 when we clapped every Thursday for the NHS? As we enter 2021, the NHS is once again our hero in this pandemic fight. A more virulent strain of the virus puts ever greater strain on the NHS.

You can help the NHS by doing your best to not get and/or spread the virus. Keep fighting the fight.

For those super heroes in the frontline battle, it is inevitable that some my lose their live, in addition to those lost on 2020. WOZiTech supports Paul & James Gilley to help say "Thank You" and remember the lives lost of those who dedicated themselves to the NHS with these amazing and poignant statues designed by Lucianne Lassalle.

We shall be donating 1% of all freelance earnings throughout 2021.

We ask all our friends, colleagues and familites, if you feel the same as us, please also contribute to this justgiving page.

Work Together. Work Smart.

Never look back with regret. But we must always look back to plan for the future. 2020, a personally tough year for all, we were supported throughout as a democratic nation. WOZiTech was lucky enough to keep working throughout 2020. Many were not.

Many businesses closed; some small, some large. In 2021, more businesses will be impacted. As small businesses, we can react faster to changing cimcumstances. As small business, we can work together to build something bigger than each of us. Like our fight against the pnademic, we must work together to forge a stronger nation.

We each have our own part to play. WOZiTech is committed developing fresh new IT talent, ready to build the next generation of IT and appplications. A workforce that is open, flexible and able to work anywhere at their own time. This is not about working hard. It"s about working smart. Be productive when you work.

We need a strong workforce. We need everyone to safely contribute to the strength of our nation. If covid-19 has taught us one thing, it's "we can do this together".
